About ECI

ESSEC Capital Investments

Building the next generation of investment professionals through hands-on equity research and rigorous financial training at ESSEC Business School.

Our Mission

Bridging theory and practice

ECI exists to bridge the gap between academic finance and real-world investment practice. We believe every ESSEC student deserves access to rigorous financial training — regardless of prior experience.

Our members develop the analytical rigour, professional communication, and collaborative mindset needed to succeed in equity research, investment banking, asset management, and related fields.

Our Vision

Excellence in student investing

We aspire to be the leading student investment association at ESSEC — recognised for the quality of our research, the calibre of our members, and the strength of our alumni network in the financial industry.

  • — Rigorous fundamental analysis on real companies
  • — Diverse sector coverage (Energy, Healthcare, Financials, Consumer, Industrials, TMT)
  • — Collaborative, mentor-driven learning environment
  • — Strong industry connections through AlumnEye
  • — A real fund to manage as we grow

What We Do

Core Activities

ECI provides a comprehensive platform for developing investment skills through hands-on, real-world practice.

Equity Research

Fundamental analysis covering financial statements, competitive positioning, and DCF / comps valuation across all major sectors.

Stock Pitches

Members present Buy / Hold / Sell recommendations backed by original financial models and published on LinkedIn for the finance community.

Fund Management

Hands-on experience managing a real portfolio with disciplined investment process, position sizing, and quarterly performance reporting.

ATP Training

Three AlumnEye-led Analyst Training Program sessions covering financial modelling, valuation, and professional pitch construction from day one.

For New Members

What You Will Learn

Whether you are a complete beginner or have finance experience, ECI structures your development.

Financial Statement Analysis
Read and interpret P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow statements. Identify key drivers and red flags in company filings.
Valuation Techniques
Build DCF models, run trading comparables (EV/EBITDA, P/E), and triangulate target prices like a sell-side analyst.
Pitch Construction
Structure and present an investment thesis — investment highlights, risks, valuation, and recommendation — in professional format.
Sector Knowledge
Deep-dive one or two sectors per year: understand industry dynamics, key metrics, and how macro conditions affect valuations.
Professional Communication
Publish equity research on LinkedIn, present in front of peers, and receive structured feedback from senior ECI members and AlumnEye coaches.
Portfolio Management
As the ECI fund grows, members gain direct experience in position sizing, portfolio construction, and performance attribution.
