Our Activities

What We Do

Three pillars that define the ECI experience — research, investing, and education.

Equity Research

Deep-dive fundamental analysis across five sector teams. Every pitch is grounded in financial modelling, valuation, and investment thesis construction.

Portfolio Management

Members manage a real simulated portfolio with disciplined position sizing, risk management, and quarterly performance reviews.

Member Training

Exclusive Analyst Training Program (ATP) sessions led by AlumnEye, internal DCF & LBO workshops, and structured peer feedback — all designed to prepare members for IB and AM recruitment.
