What to Expect

ECI runs a consistent weekly rhythm throughout the academic year.

Every week, members present and defend equity research pitches. All sectors covered. Structured Q&A and feedback from senior members.

Three sessions per year led by AlumnEye professionals — financial modelling, pitch construction, and IB / AM interview preparation.

Occasional networking events with finance alumni, end-of-semester socials, and collaborative team sessions beyond the research sessions.
