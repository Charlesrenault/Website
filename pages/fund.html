About the Fund

How It Works

The ECI Fund is a real equity portfolio managed collectively by ECI members following a disciplined investment process. Every investment decision is driven by the equity research produced in our weekly pitch sessions.

Each position originates from a formal pitch presented and challenged by the full team. The fund gives members direct, accountable portfolio management experience — something very few student associations can offer.

Investment Process

From Research to Portfolio

1. Analyst Research
Analyst produces equity research covering financials, competitive positioning, valuation, and investment thesis.
2. Pitch Presentation
Pitch presented and challenged in front of the full ECI team. Refined over one or more sessions before a vote.
3. Investment Decision
Board votes on inclusion. Position sizing determined by conviction level and portfolio construction discipline.
4. Ongoing Monitoring
Quarterly reviews with defined exit criteria. Performance reported against benchmark each quarter.
